About Me

Hello! I am Web developer graduate from General Assembly's 3 month full-time coding bootcamp.

I am super passionate about someday becoming a multi instrumentalist, but also thinking of innovative solutions to random problems I come across. I have been able to pursue music on my own spare time, but never had the knowledge or skills, nor knew where to start to build applications based of my ideas.

General Assembly has equipped me with the skills to start developing applications, but most importantly the skill to comprehend, learn and use new technologies on my own. I have since become a huge fan of web animations and JavaScript. I am excited to use these skills to start developing applications for my ideas and I also aspire to master all things animations.

I am lucky to have realised during my last job as an administration officer, that I crave challenge have a need to stimulate my mind everyday to learn something new. Now I have a vast list of things I want to work on, since I discovered the endless possibilities and projects one can do with just one programming language.

But for now, I will start diving deep into the world of animations, with canvas and GreenSock.js.
