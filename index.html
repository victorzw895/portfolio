About Me

Hello! I am Web developer graduate from General Assembly's 3 month full-time coding bootcamp.

I was previous an administration officer for Regional Express Airlines, with a background in Business Analysis. I have always been amazed by all creative things and products that people make, starting with just an idea. It has always been my dream to combine inspiration, creativity and practical skills to create anything from nothing.

General Assembly has equipped me with the skills to develop applications, but most importantly the skill to comprehend, learn and use new technologies on my own. I never thought I would one day become a developer and create things with my own abilities.

I love JavaScript and React, I am currently learning HTML5 canvas, p5.js and GreenSock.js as I am very interested in making a web application interactive with animations for a fun user experience.
